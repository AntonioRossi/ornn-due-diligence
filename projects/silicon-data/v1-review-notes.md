  and diligence list only.

  conflict governance, and market-formation proof rather than trying to tell a

- Does the opening recommendation reflect the current memo cleanly?
- Are the legal-map and regulatory contradictions represented precisely?
- Does the methodology and IOSCO scene stay specific enough without pretending
  independent validation exists?

  branding issue?

## Known Risks

- The public record is still heavily company-generated, so editorial judgment
  matters more than usual in calibrating confidence.
- The first version compresses four core memo risks into three risk panels plus
  context and closing. That tradeoff is intentional but should be reviewed.
- Any subsequent management materials may force a more explicit split between
  methodology risk and market-formation risk.
