# Compute Exchange — Fee Terms

- **URL:** https://compute.exchange/fees
- **Fetched:** 2026-04-07
- **Tool:** firecrawl_scrape
- **Reliability:** Company-generated (legal document, last updated Jan 30, 2025)

## Key facts

- **Commission:** 4% of total compute services payment from Provider to CX
- **Payment model:**
  - Contracts ≤31 days: full payment upfront
  - Contracts >31 days: initial payment (first 31 days + 10% cancellation deposit), then monthly payments
- **Customer types:**
  - Approved Customers: direct payments, must pay within 2 business days
  - Non-Approved Customers: prefund account via CX wallet, held FBO customer by third-party payment provider
- **Resale:** allowed through CX only, with premium/discount mechanics
- **No direct payments:** all payments must flow through CX platform — direct payment to provider is material breach
- **Contact:** support@tcex.com
- **GDPR:** includes EU/Swiss/UK Standard Contractual Clauses for cross-border data transfers

## Notable for analysis

- 4% take rate is modest for a marketplace
- CX holds funds "FBO" (for benefit of) via third-party payment providers — not a clearing model, but does touch money flow
- Cancellation fee structure (10% deposit) creates some economic exposure
